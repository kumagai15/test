TUAT Open Music Dataset for Research

Overview

We created forty-five pieces that consisted of melodies produced by piano sounds without harmony. The musical stimuli (MIDI) were synthesized by Sibelius (Avid Technology, USA), a music computation and notation program. The sound intensities of all of the generated musical pieces were identical. The length of each musical piece was 34 s, with the tempo set to 150 beats per minute (bpm) (i.e., the frequency of a quarter of a note was 2.5 Hz). We set the sampling frequency to 44,100 Hz.
